About Me

I’ve always believed that food is more than just a meal — it’s a universal language. It brings people together around one table, awakens all our senses, and creates lasting memories.

Growing up, I watched in wonder as my grandmother turned recipes into experiences — meals that made us pause, smile, and connect. Later, I was captivated by food programs that told stories not just through flavors, but through emotion and presentation.

That love for food is what drives my work today. I believe a restaurant’s website should feel like its signature dish: inviting, memorable, and true to its essence. And the numbers back it up: restaurants that offer online ordering on their own site see sales increase by up to 16%, and 77% of diners check a restaurant’s website before deciding where to eat.
